## Job Title

**Senior AI / Python Engineer**

## Experience

6–10+ years (with strong hands-on AI/ML experience)

## Location

## Role Overview

We are looking for a **Senior AI + Python Engineer** to design, build, and scale intelligent systems using modern AI/ML and Generative AI techniques. You will work on real-world problems involving data pipelines, model integration, RAG systems, and agent-based architectures, and help take AI solutions from prototype to production.

This role requires strong **Python engineering fundamentals** along with **practical AI/ML and LLM experience**.

---

## Key Responsibilities

* Design, develop, and maintain **AI-driven applications** using Python
* Build and optimize **machine learning and deep learning pipelines**
* Develop **Generative AI solutions** using LLMs (OpenAI, Gemini, LLaMA, etc.)
* Implement **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** systems using vector databases
* Design and orchestrate **multi-agent / agentic AI workflows** (LangChain, LangGraph, CrewAI, etc.)
* Integrate AI services with **REST APIs, databases, and cloud platforms**
* Handle **data ingestion, preprocessing, embeddings, and evaluation**
* Ensure **scalability, reliability, and observability** of AI systems
* Collaborate with product, data, and backend teams on AI features
* Mentor junior engineers and contribute to technical decisions
